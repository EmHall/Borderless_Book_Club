Borderless Book Club

"That's the thing about books; they let you travel without moving your feet" - Jhumpa Lahiri

In this book club, we will read 12 works of fiction in 12 months - each one from a different country. I have recommended a book, short story, poem and classic novel for each month, but you are welcome to foray into other genres - including film and non-fiction - and let us know what you discover.

In my research for the reading list I have been astonished by how few works considered classics overseas are even printed in English, but those that I have chosen should allow us to travel from home, and experience another way of looking at the world.

It was very difficult to choose which countries to read, but this is a good start. I have tried to ensure that the recommended reading offers representative impressions of each country and their significant neighbours.

The club will meet on the last Wednesday of every month, starting officially on 30 September. However, I will invite you to a quick introductory meeting on 26 August in case you have any questions, further book recommendations, or requests.

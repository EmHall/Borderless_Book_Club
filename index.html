Borderless Book Club 2020-2021

Travelling From Home

In this book club, we will read 12 works of literature - one each month. Texts have been selected ensure that even those of you without much time for reading will be able to participate, as you can choose from a novel, a short story, a poem or a film each month.

It has been difficult to choose the countries that we will visit on this first tour of the book club, but the aim has been to get a taste of every region outside Europe, and see things from their points of view.

If you would like to join the book club, please contact emily.hall@trade.gov.uk.
